lt;h1>Using &lt; and &gt; in HTML</h1>
lt;p>In HTML, the less-than sign is represented as &lt; and the greater-than sign is represented as &gt;.</p>
lt;p>For example:</p>
lt;pre>
amp;lt;div&gt;This is a div element&lt;/div&gt;
lt;/pre>
lt;p>This will display as: &lt;div&gt;This is a div element&lt;/div&gt;</p>
